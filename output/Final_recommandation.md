# Final Recommendation

## Executive Summary

The regression analysis was conducted to identify the key business factors influencing monthly sales across retail stores. Both Simple Linear Regression and Multiple Linear Regression models were developed and evaluated.

The Multiple Linear Regression model demonstrated superior performance because it considered several important business variables simultaneously, resulting in more accurate predictions and stronger business insights.

---

# Key Findings

The analysis indicates that monthly sales are influenced by multiple operational and customer-related factors rather than a single variable.

The most influential business drivers include:

* Marketing Spend
* Customer Footfall
* Inventory Availability
* Customer Rating
* Staff Count
* Promotional Discounts
* Store Type
* Regional Location

These variables collectively contribute to explaining variations in monthly sales.

---

# Recommended Model

**Multiple Linear Regression**

### Reason

Compared with Simple Linear Regression, the Multiple Regression model provides:

* Better prediction accuracy
* Higher explanatory power
* Improved business interpretation
* Better decision support for management

Therefore, the Multiple Linear Regression model is recommended for future sales forecasting.

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the regression analysis, the following actions are recommended:

1. Increase marketing investment in high-performing regions to maximize sales growth.

2. Improve inventory availability to reduce lost sales opportunities.

3. Focus on increasing customer footfall through targeted marketing campaigns and promotional activities.

4. Maintain high customer satisfaction by improving service quality and customer ratings.

5. Optimize staffing levels during peak business periods.

6. Use promotional discounts strategically rather than excessively to protect profit margins.

7. Continuously monitor store performance using predictive models and update the regression model periodically with new data.

---

# Expected Business Impact

Implementing these recommendations is expected to:

* Increase monthly sales
* Improve forecasting accuracy
* Enhance customer satisfaction
* Optimize operational efficiency
* Support data-driven business decisions
* Improve long-term profitability

---

# Conclusion

The regression analysis demonstrates that monthly sales are affected by a combination of marketing, operational, and customer-related factors. The Multiple Linear Regression model provides the most reliable predictions and should be adopted for future business planning, sales forecasting, and strategic decision-making.
