# Residual Analysis

## Objective

The purpose of residual analysis is to evaluate the performance of the regression model by examining the difference between the actual monthly sales and the predicted monthly sales.

---

# Residual Definition

A residual represents the prediction error of the regression model.

**Residual = Actual Sales − Predicted Sales**

A smaller residual indicates a better prediction, while a larger residual suggests that the model prediction differs significantly from the actual value.

---

# Residual Analysis Performed

The following steps were carried out during residual analysis:

* Calculated predicted monthly sales using the regression model.
* Computed residual values for each observation.
* Compared actual sales with predicted sales.
* Checked the distribution of residuals.
* Identified unusually large positive or negative residuals.

---

# Interpretation

Residual values close to zero indicate that the regression model predicts monthly sales accurately.

Positive residuals indicate that the actual sales were higher than the predicted sales.

Negative residuals indicate that the predicted sales were higher than the actual sales.

A random distribution of residuals around zero suggests that the regression model fits the data well and that no systematic prediction errors are present.

---

# Model Evaluation

The residual analysis indicates that the regression model provides a reliable estimation of monthly sales for most observations.

No consistent pattern was observed in the residual values, suggesting that the assumptions of linear regression are reasonably satisfied.

---

# Business Interpretation

The regression model can be used to estimate future monthly sales based on key business factors such as marketing spend, footfall, customer rating, inventory availability, and store characteristics.

Stores with larger residual values may require additional investigation, as external factors not included in the model may be influencing sales performance.

---

# Conclusion

Residual analysis confirms that the regression model performs satisfactorily and is suitable for business forecasting and decision-making. While minor prediction errors exist, the overall model demonstrates acceptable predictive performance and can support strategic planning.
